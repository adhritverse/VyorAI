(1) Virtual Try-On API for Businesses

Our Virtual Try-On feature is available as a business API.

As the name suggests, businesses can integrate this API into their platforms to allow customers to virtually try on clothing before making a purchase. This enhances user experience, reduces uncertainty, and improves conversion rates.

(2) Infinity (Plugin SDK)

Infinity is our core Plugin SDK designed for developers.

Developers can log in, generate an API key, and integrate our plugin directly into their websites. Once installed, the plugin appears seamlessly within the site interface.

When users interact with it, they can discover products more intelligently. For example, on traditional e-commerce platforms like Amazon, finding the right product often requires checking ratings, reviews, and multiple filters—taking 8–10 clicks on average.

With Infinity, powered by our in-house LLM, users simply describe what they want in natural language and instantly get the most relevant results—making product discovery faster, smarter, and more intuitive.
